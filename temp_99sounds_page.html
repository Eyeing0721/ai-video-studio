    Cinematic Sounds

    By 99SoundsOctober 23, 2025Updated:April 29, 202621 Comments3 Mins Read

    Cinematic Sounds is a free collection of cinematic sound effects and cinematic loops from Flame Sound’s commercial releases, now available exclusively on 99Sounds.

    The pack contains 99 high-quality royalty-free sounds, including braams, booms, impacts, whooshes, tension builders, cinematic loops, and more. Every sound was carefully designed, edited, and mastered to be production-ready and fit seamlessly into your creative workflow.

    The free download link is located at the end of this article.

    FREE Cinematic Sounds

    Cinematic Sounds delivers a curated selection of 99 premium-quality cinematic sound effects from Flame Sound, my professional sound design label.

    These are the same types of sounds used in my commercial cinematic libraries and are now available for free for the 99Sounds community.

    The collection covers the essential building blocks of cinematic sound design, from braams for low-end impact and tension to full cinematic loops for adding atmosphere and rhythm to your projects.

    All sounds are 100% royalty-free and can be used in music production, sound design, film scoring, trailer soundtracks, video games, and any other creative project.

    The content is sorted into directories by type, so you can easily browse and find the right sounds for your project. All sounds are provided in 24-bit WAV format and are compatible with popular music production and editing software.

    From Flame Sound to 99Sounds

    Flame Sound is my premium cinematic sound design label, focused on delivering professional-grade effects for composers and sound designers.

    For this special release, I’ve curated a small but carefully selected collection of premium sounds to share for free with the 99Sounds community.

    I’ve been doing sound design for over 15 years, and it’s an absolute pleasure to give something back to the creative community that supported me from the very beginning.

    99Sounds started as a passion project 15 years ago, and I’m proud that it’s still alive and kicking today. I’ll continue to add high-quality content to help it grow, knowing that 99Sounds remains one of the most popular sources of free sounds online.

    If you’d like to explore more cinematic sounds in a similar style, you can find my full commercial sound libraries over at Flame Sound.

    Details

    Cinematic Sounds contains 99 cinematic sound effects and loops in 24-bit WAV format (48 kHz). It includes 357 MB of royalty-free audio content.

    • 99 cinematic sound effects & loops
    • Categories: Braams, Booms, Impacts, Textures, Whooshes, Tension Builders, Loops
    • 24-bit WAV format (compatible with all editing software)
    • Royalty-free for commercial use
    • Curated from Flame Sound’s premium libraries

    The library offers a free taste of Flame Sound’s premium cinematic sound design. It’s perfect for composers, trailer producers, game developers, and sound designers who want to add cinematic impact to their projects.
